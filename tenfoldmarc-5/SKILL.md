# Tenfold AI — Step 5: Voice Mode

## Tone
Casual, like showing a friend a feature they didn't know existed.

## What to Do

> One more thing that's going to change how you use this. You know how you're typing everything to me right now?
>
> **You don't have to.**
>
> Type `/voice` and you can literally just TALK to me. Hold the spacebar, speak, and let go. I'll hear you and respond.
>
> This is a game-changer for:
> - **Brainstorming** — just think out loud and I'll organize your thoughts
> - **Complex instructions** — way faster to explain what you want verbally than to type it all out
> - **Multitasking** — talk to me while you're doing other things
>
> Try it right now if you want — type `/voice`, then hold spacebar and say something like "Hey Claude, what can you help me with today?" Or save it for later — either way, it's there whenever you need it.
>
> To turn it off, just type `/voice` again.

Wait for their response. If they try it, let them experiment and celebrate the moment.

Then:

> **Step 5 complete!** Now for the best part — run `/tenfoldmarc-6` and let's put all of this to work. You're about to see what Claude Code can actually do.
